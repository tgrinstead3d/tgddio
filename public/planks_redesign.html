Since 1939

Long before German Village was the trendy spot it is today, Walter Plank opened his doors on Parsons Avenue.

Through prohibition, regeneration, and generations of change, one thing has stayed the same: Plank's is where neighbors become friends. Whether you're here for a quick lunch or a long evening on the patio, you're part of our history.
